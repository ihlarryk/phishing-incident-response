# Step 5: Incident Response

## Immediate Actions (First 24 Hours)

| Priority | Action | Why |
|----------|--------|-----|
| 1 | Disconnect file server from network | Stop ransomware spread |
| 2 | Reset all Microsoft 365 passwords | Revoke attacker access |
| 3 | Disable compromised employee account | Block entry point |
| 4 | Preserve phishing email as evidence | Needed for investigation |
| 5 | Check which files were encrypted | Assess damage |
| 6 | Notify management | Legal and business decisions |

## Legal Requirement (Germany)
Under GDPR Article 33, if personal data was compromised, FiktivGmbH must notify the Datenschutzbehörde within 72 hours or face fines up to €20 million or 4% of annual revenue.
